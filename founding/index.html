FOR ACADEMIA While researchers are most welcome to present their work at EHSM, please note that this is not a traditional academic conference. We will not formally publish proceedings(*), and we do not claim affiliation with any institution. We are also OK with previously published work, we simply expect quality presentations. The main goals of EHSM are to share knowledge, learn about new fields, and meet like-minded people - not to increase one's h-index or anything like that.
(*) Contrary to the practice of most academic publishers, we will, however, do our best to ensure the free dissemination of information.
